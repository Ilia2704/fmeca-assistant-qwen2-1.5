FMECA Workflow:

Step 1 – Preparation:
Define objectives, scope, operating modes, environment.

Step 2 – Functional Analysis:
Identify functions for each system element.

Step 3 – Failure Identification:
List potential failure modes per function.

Step 4 – Effects Analysis:
Identify consequences at multiple levels.

Step 5 – Cause Analysis:
Identify mechanical, electrical, software, human, and environmental causes.

Step 6 – Controls:
Classify controls as preventive, detective, or mitigating.

Step 7 – Risk Assessment:
Assign S, O, D values.

Step 8 – Actions:
Define corrective or preventive actions with owners and deadlines.
