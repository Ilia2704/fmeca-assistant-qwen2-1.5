Basic FMECA Rules:

1. Define scope and system boundaries.
2. Identify system structure and functions.
3. Identify failure modes for each function.
4. Describe effects at:
   - local level
   - system level
   - end-user / safety / business level
5. Identify causes and existing controls.
6. Assign S, O, D using consistent scales.
7. Prioritize actions, not numbers.
8. Document assumptions and uncertainties.
