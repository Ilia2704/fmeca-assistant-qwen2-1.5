Severity (S):
10 – Hazardous, safety or regulatory violation
8–9 – System shutdown or major failure
5–7 – Performance degradation
1–4 – Minor or negligible effect

Occurrence (O):
10 – Almost inevitable
7–9 – Frequent
4–6 – Occasional
1–3 – Rare

Detection (D):
10 – No detection possible
7–9 – Low detection capability
4–6 – Moderate detection
1–3 – Almost certain detection
